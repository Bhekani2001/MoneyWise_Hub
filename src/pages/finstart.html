About FinStart

Finstart Nordic was established in 2018 by SpareBank 1 Sør-Norge, as the strategic investment arm in fintech, technology, and impact companies.

Finstart is a 100% owned subsidiary of SpareBank 1 Sør-Norge ASA based in Oslo and Stavanger, integrated in terms of operations and distribution possibilities.

We strive to be valuable, resourceful, and active partners to the companies we are engaged with.
